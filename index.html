Meet Svetlana.

Svetlana Saitsky is a certified executive coach, facilitator, and Mental Wealth advocate. With 17+ years across Tech, Finance, Life Sciences, and Education, she has coached leaders at Google, Apple, Airbnb, Netflix, Asana, Square, and more.

She is a certified Ambassador of Compassion through Stanford Medical School's Center for Compassion and Altruism Research — and a lifelong artist, hat maker, and radically poetic human being who has lived many lives in one.

She built an entire career around the one skill nobody was ever taught. This is that school.
